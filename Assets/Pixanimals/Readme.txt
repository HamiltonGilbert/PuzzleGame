Cute pet pack.
     What you get:

-  cute pet animal sprites.

- each with animations such as ( walk , death , hurt, attack  , sleep , blinking and idle)

-  There is 4 pet animals in this pack , e.g. Cat , turtle , bunny and a frog (cute one).

- All pet animals are in 32x32 size except cat , the cat is 48x48.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
